How to apply for a resident in Turkey?
First, you apply for an appointment in the immigration office by yourself on https://e-ikamet.goc.gov.tr,then you apply for your Resident

Duration to get your resident after appointment in one month maximum.

What Documents need to prepare for Resident Appointment?

- Rental House Agreement with noter stamp. - Personal Photo With white background. - Passport Photo. - Taxes number. - For Children under 18 years old, Birth certificate should be stamped from your foreign ministry then Turkish embassy.

Birth certificate to stamp in Turkey, First stamp it in your embassy in Ankara then Translate it to Turkish and stamp it in Turkish Foreign Ministry.
