Ceramic,Bathroom & kitchen Equipments Fair

5 - 9 February 2019 Venue: CNR Expo Yeşilköy – İstanbul Turkey
05-02-19

One of the two biggest ceramics, bathroom and kitchen exhibitions in the world, UNICERA Istanbul, will take place on 160 thousand square meters area at CNR EXPO on 5-9 February 2019 offering an export potential worth millions of dollars. UNICERA that also carries the distinction of being Turkey's most comprehensive specialized exhibition will be organized with the goal of receiving 100 thousand professional visitors from 4 continents of which 20 thousand would be international. Professional buyers from 128 countries, including the USA, Germany, Italy, France, England, Spain, Poland, Bulgaria, Macedonia, Russia, Georgia, Libya, Morocco, Algeria, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Iran, and Israel will hold bilateral talks with the exhibitors.

UNICERA, where new products and models will come together with the world ceramics industry, supplies the ceramics, bath and kitchen requirements of Europe, America, Africa, the Middle East and the Commonwealth of Independent States. CNR Expo where UNICERA is organized to unite over 1,000 brands with international buyers each year, is known as the "Ceramics Trade Center" for Turkey and neighboring countries. Exhibitors of UNICERA besides supplying to the Turkey market meets ceramics, bathroom, and kitchen needs of the Middle East, Central Asia, Europe, North Africa, the Commonwealth of the Independent States
